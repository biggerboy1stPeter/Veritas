Dear esteemed scholars, researchers, and prospective students,

I warmly welcome you to the College of Postgraduate Studies, Veritas University, Abuja. Our postgraduate programs are designed to meet global standards, combining theoretical depth with practical relevance. Through dedicated faculty, robust curricula, and a growing research culture, we aim to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to contribute meaningfully to academia, industry, governance, and society at large.

We assure you of our unwavering commitment to quality teaching, effective supervision, timely program delivery, and continuous improvement.

Prospective and returning postgraduate students, researchers, and partners are invited to explore the opportunities available.

Prof. Ikechukwu Anthony Kanu, OSA
